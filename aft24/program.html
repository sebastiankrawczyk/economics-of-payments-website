EoP XII — Program

The Federal Reserve Board will host the Economics of Payments XII Conference in Washington, D.C., on October 26 and 27, 2023. This in-person conference offers an opportunity for economists, academics, central bankers, and policymakers to present and discuss work on payments, financial market infrastructures (FMIs), and related topics. If you have questions, please contact the conference organizers at economicsofpayments@frb.gov.
