Economics of Payments XIII

The Oesterreichische Nationalbank (Central Bank of Austria) is pleased to host the Economics of Payments XIII conference. This conference offers an opportunity for economists, central bankers, and policymakers to present and discuss their studies on topics related to payment, clearing and settlement systems.

Keynote presentations will be given by Piero Cipollone, Member of the Executive Board of the European Central Bank and Chairman of the High-Level Task Force on the Digital Euro, and by Jean Charles Rochet, Toulouse School of Economics.

The inaugural day will include collaborative sessions with the Advances in Financial Technologies conference, aimed at promoting a multidisciplinary dialogue on the technological facets of payment innovations. This day will feature keynotes by Neha Narula from the Digital Currency Initiative at MIT and Charles M. Kahn from the University of Illinois and Bank of Canada. The day will conclude with a panel discussion, an evening reception, and a poster session.
